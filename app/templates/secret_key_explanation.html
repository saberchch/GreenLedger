Understanding Your Secret Key

Your Secret Key

Your secret key is a crucial part of your account security. It is used to encrypt your sensitive information and should be kept confidential.

Your Secret Key:

How to Keep Your Secret Key Safe

  • Do not share your secret key with anyone.
  • Store it in a secure location, such as a password manager.
  • Do not write it down in easily accessible places.
  • Be cautious of phishing attempts that may try to trick you into revealing your secret key.

If you lose your secret key, you may lose access to your account and its associated data.
